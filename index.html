What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well being. A persons' mental health determines how they cope with stress, relate to others, make choices, and more.

Why is Good Mental Health Important?

Good mental health is essential for a good quality of life and overall well being. You can improve your mental health by meditating, mindfulness activities, or by doing anything soothing that you enjoy. On this website, you try out various meditation and mental health activities.
